Alex Tomson
Who could imagine...

At the time of Druids and Fairys, when everybody was resting, a thunder interupted their sleep. He was born, under the stars, in the middle of the night, among the animals which protected him. It was obvious, he was meant to be something different, something extraordinary had happened.

He was raised by the wild, in forests having wizards and Fairys for friends.

At the age of 15 something happened that changed his life forever. He met the Great Master of Azarot (a small village), who meant to be his mentor.

It was he, the Great Master who introduced the young wilding to the civilization.

The rest is a common story. School, college, university, girls, drinks ... (that's all I can say...)
